About Me

Composer & Musician based in NYC

LA born, NYC based VINNI is a producer, composer, singer, pianist, and jazz saxophonist. He began experimenting as a music major studying at Columbia University, where he composed, performed and produced his first full length jazz album entitled "Acacia" in 2019. He then went on to compose and produce a six movement classical chamber music album entitled the "COLORS project" in 2020, naming the pieces after the primary and secondary colors.

VINNI's latest singles focus on producing alternative lo fi beats underneath reflective and contemplative lyrics on the human experience. Inspired by artists such as keshi, HONNE, Lauv, VINNI attempts to grapple with the tribulations of young love, loss, and identity. His music explores the raw emotions stemming from the pain and grief behind having to accept and let go of a loved one. While everyone has their own interpretation of love and loss, VINNI hopes to convey a small facet of his experience that any listener can relate to.
